About Jarvis.

This my morning default application. Design question here is not how it looks like, or how it sounds like. The question is what do you want to hear in the morning when you open your computer.

Currently it gathers and reads the following:

  • Greetings.
  • Time and date.
  • Calendar events.
  • To-do list (from iCal).
  • Weather conditions. (for the moment this is not working)
  • Unread email count and VIP email count.
  • Location of tracked packages. (for the moment this is not working)
  • News headlines from NYTimes.
  • Daily quotations.
For obvious reasons it is called Jarvis.
